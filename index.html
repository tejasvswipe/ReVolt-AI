The Challenge

A growing crisis hidden in our drawers.

India is one of the world's largest generators of electronic waste — yet most of it is mishandled, exported informally, or simply forgotten. Behind every discarded device is also a quiet data-security risk.

India's E-Waste Problem

Millions of tonnes of electronics are discarded each year. Collection is fragmented, recycling informal, and recovery rates remain low. The result: toxic leakage, lost materials, missed economic value.

Hidden Data Risk

Every laptop, phone, and server holds sensitive data. When devices are resold, donated, or scrapped, that data often goes with them — exposing individuals, businesses, and institutions to breach.

How AI Changes It

AI can coordinate pickup logistics, identify devices, classify components, and trace materials through the recycling chain — turning a messy, opaque flow into something measurable and trustworthy.

A Circular Vision

We imagine a nationwide ecosystem where electronics are collected responsibly, data is destroyed verifiably, and materials flow back into new devices — a true circular economy for India's digital life.

What is ReVolt AI?

ReVolt AI is an early-stage startup building an AI-powered network for responsible electronics disposal and secure data destruction in India. We're in validation phase — sharing the vision and gathering input from future users, companies, investors, and recycling partners.

When will services launch?

We're being deliberately careful. Our first public step will be a small pilot collection network in a single city. A broader launch timeline will follow once we've validated the model in the field. Join the waitlist to be notified.

Which devices will be accepted?

Our initial scope focuses on the highest-volume IT categories: laptops, desktops, mobile devices, monitors, networking equipment, and enterprise servers. A full device list will be published when the pilot opens.

How will data destruction work?

We'll combine certified software-based wiping with chain-of-custody tracking and, where appropriate, physical destruction. Every device will come with verifiable proof of destruction — documentation your compliance and security teams can rely on.

How can businesses partner?

We're selectively talking to IT, sustainability, and procurement leaders who want to shape the early product. If your organization is thinking about asset disposal at scale, we'd love to hear from you — please reach out via the contact form.
